FAQs

The duration of Rishihood University's Bachelor of Design Residential Program is 4 years.
The B.Design course tuition fee at Rishihood University is Rs. 4.2 Lakh per year

Choosing the best specialization in B.Des depends on your interests and future career goals. At Rishihood University's School of Design, each program is designed to prepare students for high-growth industries and future jobs in design:

UX & Interaction Design (UXID): Careers in UX/UI design, digital products, AR/VR, gaming, and AI-driven experiences.

Communication Design: Opportunities in branding, advertising, visual communication, motion graphics, and digital media strategy.

Product Design: Careers in industrial design, furniture, packaging, wearable technology, and sustainable product innovation.

Every specialization has strong career scope in the 21st century—the "best" choice is the one aligned with your passion and skills. At Rishihood University, our mentors guide students to explore these design fields and build impactful, future-ready careers.

Yes, we have scholarships available in 4 categories.

Vidya Shree: Merit-based scholarships given to top scorers of entrance exams.

Next-Gen Women Leader: Available to problem-solving meritorious students with excellent leadership potential

Pragya Jyoti: Another Merit-based scholarship offered to needy students with excellent academic records.

Vividha Samman: A scholarship that gives a platform to talented students in design, visual and fine arts, sports or literature

Graduates of Rishihood's B.Des program work with tech firms, design studios, startups, and creative agencies. Entry-level salaries typically range from ₹4–8 LPA with many also pursuing freelance work or launching their own design ventures.

About Us

Rishihood University is India's first and only Impact University, dedicated to nurturing leaders who drive meaningful change. Founded by a collective of scholars, mentors, and changemakers, Rishihood offers an education that is Indian in spirit, global in outlook, and future-ready shaping learners into impactful leaders who embody the essence of 'Rishihood'.
